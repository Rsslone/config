#Information about Hunger
Nutrition adds the idea of food groups to minecraft, eating a balanced diet will grant you an advantage, a poor diet however can be deadly. To access your current levels, press the &3CARROT &ricon in your inventory screen.

&a85% for each group&r adds +1 level of Toughness (Increased Armor, Health, Attack Speed)

&a15% for each group&r adds +1 level of Malnourished (Reduced Health)

&a15% Average&r of all groups gives Mining Fatigue (Slow mining and attack)

&a5% Average&r of all groups gives Starving (Hunger Effect)

&a1% in all&r groups will give Wither &c(You will die)
